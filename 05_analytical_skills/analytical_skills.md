Five Key Analytical Skills I Have Learned

1\. Curiosity



Asking deeper questions to understand the real problem.



2\. Understanding the Context



Knowing the environment, people, or business situation behind the data.



3\. Technical Mindset



Breaking problems down into steps and thinking logically.



4\. Data Design



Knowing how information should be organized.



5\. Data Strategy



Planning how data will be collected, used, stored, and maintained.



These skills help analysts make better judgments using data and intuition together.
